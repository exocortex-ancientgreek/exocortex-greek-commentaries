Commentary

1Ἐτυγχάνομεν we happened to; governs a complementary pple translated as an inf.
2ἐν ᾧ in which; relative pronoun, note the aspiration
3τοῦ νεώ of the temple; obj. of ἔμπροσθεν
4ἦν (there was); 3rd sg. impf. εἰμί
4καί joining modifiers ξένη and ἔχουσα
5οὓς…τίνες…ἦσαν what in the world these in fact are; “these…what in the world (they) are”; οὓς is a relative pronoun better translated as a demonstrative (these)
5ἠδυνάμεθα indicative is past potential
6οὓς…τίνες…ἦσαν what in the world these in fact are; “these…what in the world (they) are”; οὓς is a relative pronoun better translated as a demonstrative (these)
6εἶναι inf. εἰμί, complement of ἐδόκει
7γεγραμμένον the drawing; “thing drawn,” pf. pass. pple γράφω is nom. subject
8τὸν μὲν…τὸν δέ one…and the other; in apposition to περιβόλους δύο
9μείζω, ἐλάττω i.e. acc. sg. of the comparatives μείζο(ν)α and ἐλάττο(ν)α
9τὸν μὲν…τὸν δέ one…and the other; in apposition to περιβόλους δύο
9καί also; adverbial
9ἐπὶ upon; i.e. placed within the wall
10πρὸς near; + dat. place where
11ἐφεστάναι to be standing beside; “to have stood near,” pf. inf. ἐφίστημι
12τι a certain; modifies the neuter πλῆθος
12ἑωρᾶτο was seen; ἑωράετο impf. ὁράω
12ἐπὶ upon; presumably on the wall over the gateway rather than at the gateway
13ἐφεστώς nom. sg. pf. pple ἐφίστημι
14ὡς as if; with nom. sg. pple; ὡς + pple often expresses alleged cause or intent
14τι a certain (order); inner accusative
14εἰσιόντι dat. sg. pres. pple εἰσέρχομαι
