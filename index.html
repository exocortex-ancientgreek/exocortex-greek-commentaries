Exocortex — Ancient Greek commented editions

Commented editions of Greek texts — the running vocabulary and commentary of their editors, turned into something you can actually read on a screen.

52 editions · 61,318 lines · 95,193 notes

The text and the commentary are not mine. They were written by the editors below and published free under CC BY-NC-SA. All I did was turn a printed page into a reading interface: click a word to see its note, keep the page's vocabulary beside the text, and hand word analysis to Alpheios.

If you find these useful, go to the editors first — buy the print edition or send them something. Their links are next to each group.
